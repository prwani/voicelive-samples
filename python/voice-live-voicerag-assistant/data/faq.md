# FAQ

## How to change or recover my ContosoBank password?

**Question:** How can I change or recover my ContosoBank password if I forgot it?

**Answer:** To change or recover your password, follow these steps:

1. Open the ContosoBank app. If you're logged in, log out first
2. Enter your email address or SSN/Tax ID and click Continue
3. Click "Forgot password"
4. Follow the app instructions to send your selfie
5. Create your new password following the required criteria and click Continue
6. Enter the new password again and click Confirm password

**Important:**
- Passwords must have 6 digits
- Don't reuse previous passwords or use easy sequences
- If locked due to excessive attempts, wait 30 minutes
- When resetting, your access expires on all devices
- Re-enable biometrics after changing your password

If you cannot change it through the app, contact Customer Service.

## I forgot the email to access my account. What now?

**Question:** What to do if I forgot the email registered to my account?

**Answer:** If you forgot the email and cannot log in:

1. Go to the ContosoBank website and click "Access my Account"
2. Enter your SSN or Tax ID

If you have multiple accounts, the emails will be listed in masked format for security.

If you still cannot remember the email, contact your provider to request access using your registered information.

## How to redeem my investment?

**Reading time:** 1 minute

Those who invest in one of ContosoBank's CD options can redeem the invested amount before the maturity date, right after the grace period.

### Available options:

- Redeem all the money on the same day as the investment
- Redeem all money after the grace period and before maturity
- Redeem part of the money after the grace period and before maturity
- Redeem all money at maturity (the amount will be automatically available in your ContosoBank account)

### How to redeem:

1. Open the ContosoBank app using login and password
2. Go to the Investments tab and click My investments
3. Select the investment you want to redeem by clicking Show details and then Redeem
4. Enter the amount you want to return to your ContosoBank account
5. Review the information and confirm the redemption

### Important information:

**Redemptions Monday through Thursday:**
- Request between 6am and 10pm: money is automatically deposited into the account
- Outside this period: redemption is scheduled for the next day

**Redemptions Friday through Sunday:**
- Request from 6am Friday until 10pm Sunday: amount transferred automatically
- Outside this period: scheduled for the next day

**Additional notes:**
- CDs without daily liquidity after the grace period have automatic redemption on the business day following maturity
- For same-day redemptions with total invested value over $5,000 (in BancoSeguro CDs, Piggy Bank, and Auto Save), the request must be made by 6pm

## How to activate or configure the card reader?

**Reading time:** 1 minute

The first step to start selling with the card reader is to activate/configure it. This process is done only before first use and ensures the security of your data.

Follow the steps according to the model:

### Minizinha | Minizinha NFC

1. Turn on the machine by pressing the button for a few seconds
2. On your phone or tablet, enable Bluetooth
3. Open the ContosoBank app and click on the Sales tab
4. Click Manage sales and then ContosoBank Card Readers
5. Click Activate Minizinha and select the model to be activated
6. Press the 0 button on the machine to see the ID and click Yes, I can see it
7. Finish by clicking the green button

### Minizinha Chip | Moderninha Plus | Moderninha Pro | Moderninha Smart | Moderninha Wi-Fi | Moderninha X

1. Turn on the machine by pressing the button for a few seconds
2. After the welcome screen, press the button to continue
3. Scan the QR Code with your phone camera or enter the activation code manually
4. Press the button to confirm activation

### Additional information:

- You can check the activation code in 4 ways: via SMS, email, ContosoBank app, or ContosoBank website (by accessing your account)
- To check the code through your ContosoBank account, you need to complete security validation via QR Code
- If the screen locks during the process, press the power button to continue
- If you're having difficulties, see the article "I'm having problems activating the machine. What should I do?"

## How to advance the value of an installment sale?

**Reading time:** 3 minutes

By contracting sales advance (or receivables), you can sell in installments to your customers but choose to receive the amount all at once. This process can be done directly through the app, website, or card reader. Here's how:

### Via app:

1. Open the ContosoBank app using login and password
2. In the bottom menu, go to the Sales option and then click on the Manage sales tab
3. Click on Receivables Advance and check the amount available for advancement
4. In the upper corner, tap Search values and choose which card readers you want to advance sales from. At this point, you can customize the amounts to be received
5. Check the displayed information, such as amount to advance, the corresponding advance fee, and the net amount to be received. If correct, continue with the process
6. Tap Confirm advance and enter the code received via SMS on your secure phone to confirm the advance
7. Done! Your advance is in progress and the payment deadline will be displayed on the screen

### Via website:

1. Log into your account through the website
2. Click Sales in the left side menu, then Receivables advance
3. At the top of the screen, click Search values and select the institutions and amount you want to advance
4. Check the information such as amount to advance, the corresponding advance fee, and the net amount to be received. If correct, continue with the process
5. Click Confirm advance and enter the SMS received on your secure phone to confirm the advance
6. Done! Your advance is in progress and the payment deadline will be displayed on the screen

### Via card reader (Moderninha Pro and Pro 2)

1. Go to the menu and click My Business
2. Select the Receivables Advance option and choose the amount you want to advance
3. Check the conditions and the fee charged for the advance. Confirm by clicking Continue
4. Check the destination account and the amount to be transferred. Click Confirm

### Important information:

- The advance service contract must be made through service channels, Monday through Friday, between 8:30am and 7pm for ContosoBank machines and from 8:30am to 4pm for other machines
- Service contracting is subject to credit analysis
- Advance can only be made if there is available balance to receive
- Sales made on the day are available for advance only on the next business day. For example, sales made on 05/04 will be available for advance on 05/05. Remember that only credit sales (cash or installments) are eligible for advance. This advance deadline does not affect your payment plan
- You can make up to one advance per day. Remember that after the request, it is not possible to cancel the process
- Machines enabled with the advance feature: Moderninha Pro, Moderninha Pro 2, and Moderninha Pro (V240 model)
- The advance fee varies according to your plan and amounts to be received. This fee will always be informed at the time of request
- For same-day receipt of the advance on ContosoBank machines, the request must be made before 2pm (Bras√≠lia time) and until 11:30am for other machines
- The feature is available in the app only in updated versions
